What research are companies doing?

Governments and most of the large or well known tech organisations have spent huge sums of money on this technology. Nature reports that Quantum computing companies received over half a billion in private funding between 2017 and 2018. These are very substantial numbers for such an immature field.

Company What are they doing?
IBM IBM have already have a quantum computer that they named "IBM-Q System One".
Google Researchers from Google recently claimed to have reached quantum supremacy solving a calculation in 3 minutes that would have taken our best classical computer 10,000 years, but the paper was redacted very quickly.
Microsoft Microsoft are planning on taking Quantum Computing to the cloud and offer businesses and individuals Quantum power.
Intel intel Labs are developing production level quantum processors which they plan to release within the decade.
